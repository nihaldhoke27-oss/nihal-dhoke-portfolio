Research Insights

Top Findings from
the LinkedIn Research

10 evidence-based insights from analyzing the world's top B2B LinkedIn creators

01

Founder Content = 8–10× Company Page

Founder LinkedIn posts receive 8–10× more engagement per impression than equivalent company page posts.

Chris Walker / Refine Labs
02

Dark Social Hides 40–60% of Pipeline

LinkedIn-influenced pipeline shows as "Direct" in CRM. Self-reported attribution reveals the true source.

Refine Labs Research
03

The 90-Day Compound Curve

Consistent posting for 90 days builds algorithmic trust. Most creators quit on day 73 — right before compound interest kicks in.

Justin Welsh, Lara Acosta
04

Specificity is a Growth Multiplier

"I help B2B SaaS CMOs generate pipeline from LinkedIn" attracts exactly the right people at scale.

Lara Acosta
05

Comments Outweigh Likes 10:1

LinkedIn's algorithm weights comments dramatically higher than likes. Design posts to invite debate, not approval.

LinkedIn Algorithm Research
06

Never Put Links in Post Body

External links reduce organic reach by 50–80%. Always put links in the first comment.

Justin Welsh, Algorithm Docs
07

Write Your Hook Last

The best hooks are distillations of the completed post — written last, not first. Spend 50% of editing time on line 1.

Jasmin Alić
08

LinkedIn Seeds Every Other Channel

In every case study — LinkedIn organic content was the foundational channel that seeded newsletters, communities, and revenue.

Multiple Case Studies
